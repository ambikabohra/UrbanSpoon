URBANSPOON

RESTAURANTS' TRACKER WEBSITE

Multiple websites are available today for food ordering and restaurant recommendation. There is no website which provides suggestions about establishing a new restaurant. Restaurants should adapt to the choices of the people for a profitable business. Before opening a new restaurant, one should check which cuisine best works for that place. The restaurant owner must have an outlook of competitors.

Product objectives: The main objective of UrbanSpoon is to provide meaningful statistics based on the analysis of existing restaurants by comparing their price ranges, cuisine types, locations and ratings. This helps people willing to start a new restaurant or food chain.
